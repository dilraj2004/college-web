Welcome to City of Glasgow College

City of Glasgow College is Scotland's largest technical and professional skills college. Its award winning twin-site super campus in the heart if Glasgow offers outstanding resources and opportunities for students to gain essential skills for their future careers.

Innovative approaches to teaching and learning enable personalised development across a range of subjects on campus and online. Up to 8,000 graduates leave the college each year for the world of work, with over 90% of successful students progressing directly to further learning, apprenticeships or employment.
